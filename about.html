Who we are

GEMIS is a software development studio from South Africa, building for the world. We design, engineer and ship platforms that carry real operations — payroll runs, waste collections, learning journeys, brand launches — and we stay until they work beautifully.

Small team, senior hands

No hand-offs to juniors after the pitch. The people who scope your product are the people who build it.

Products, not projects

We measure ourselves on what happens after launch — adoption, uptime and how much your team can do without us.

Design and code, one room

UX and engineering sit together from day one, so what gets designed is what ships — pixel for pixel.

( How we think )

Four habits that shape everything we ship.

01

Understand before you build

Discovery is not a phase to rush — it's where the product is actually won or lost.

02

Momentum is a feature

Working software in weeks. Feedback loops over roadmaps, deploys over decks.

03

Craft survives contact

Typed, tested, observable systems — because real users are less polite than demos.

04

Leave teams stronger

Documentation, guidelines and codebases your own people are proud to inherit.

( The journey )

From a two-person dev shop to a full-stack studio.

  1. 2018

    GEMIS is born

    Two people, one laptop each, and a conviction that local businesses deserved world-class software.

  2. 2020

    First platforms ship

    Early client systems go live — and the studio learns its defining lesson: understand the operation first.

  3. 2022

    ManaGem takes shape

    Everything learned from client work converges into one product — an operating system for growing businesses.

  4. 2024

    Brand Studio opens

    Identity design joins the bench, led by Sphamandla Xaba — because great products deserve great brands.

  5. Today

    Building what's next

    Platforms, mobile apps, AI systems and brands — one studio, end to end, for teams across the map.
