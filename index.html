The studio

GEMIS is a team of engineers and designers who turn complex problems into elegant software. We architect, build and ship digital products that feel effortless — and scale without fear.

Precision first

Every system we ship is typed, tested and observable. Craftsmanship is not a luxury — it is how software survives contact with the real world.

Speed with intent

We move in tight iterations, deploying from day one. Momentum is a feature: our clients see working software in weeks, not quarters.

Partners, not vendors

We embed with your team, share our playbooks and leave you stronger — with codebases your own engineers love to inherit.
