( Our development process )

How we deliver successful projects — six stages, no surprises.

  1. 01

    Discovery & Business Analysis

    We immerse ourselves in your operations to understand your challenges, goals and opportunities before proposing anything.

  2. 02

    Solution Architecture

    We design a secure, scalable Microsoft Cloud architecture that fits your business today — and where it's heading.

  3. 03

    UX & System Design

    Wireframes and interactive prototypes make sure the system works the way your people actually do.

  4. 04

    Agile Development

    Iterative sprints with regular demonstrations keep you involved and the solution on target throughout the build.

  5. 05

    Quality Assurance

    Structured testing, performance validation and user acceptance before anything reaches production.

  6. 06

    Deployment & Continuous Support

    Careful rollout, training and documentation — then ongoing monitoring, maintenance and improvement.
